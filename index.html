Vision & Mission

Vision:
Empowering GSB’s research and learning community with deep expertise, innovative services, and outstanding collections, and enabling the officers and staffs of GSB to engage optimally with the ever changing information environment.

Mission:
GSB Library's mission is to support the office community in developing skills in innovative and analytical thinking in research and in information literacy, and by providing distinctive collections, services, facilities and programs.

Library Objectives

To support and facilitate the research, learning and administrative activities of the organization, by organizing, maintaining and providing access to appropriate literature and information resources in such a way as to provide optimum benefit for Library users. To provide an appropriate and comfortable environment, accommodation and facilities for the use of Library resources. To support the department emerge as a leading geo-scientific library of the country, embracing traditional values of higher education but adapted to the global community of the 21st century.To provide and ensure quality services and good professional practice in library activities.
